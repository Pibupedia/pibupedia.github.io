Nothing is lost in this world, only found in different ways. That's how life works, eh?" speaker="Artis" %}**Artis the Mantis** is the [Explorers' Association](/Explorers'_Association) accountant. He gives [Team Snakemouth](/Team_Snakemouth) the [Hard Mode](/Hard_Mode) [Medal](/Medals) and will reward them for defeating certain enemies while it's equipped.

If a boss is not defeated in [Hard Mode](/Hard_Mode) the first time it is encountered, its reward must instead be bought from the [caravan](/Traveling_Caravan).
