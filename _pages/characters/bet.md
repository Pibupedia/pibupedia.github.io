**Bet** is a character in *[Bug Fables: The Everlasting Sapling](/Bug_Fables:_The_Everlasting_Sapling)*. He is the receptionist of the [Colosseum](/Colosseum) in [Termite City](/Termite_City). He helps Team Snakemouth enter the tournament to prove their worth in the [Termite Kingdom](/Termite_Kingdom).
